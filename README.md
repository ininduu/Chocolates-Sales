# 🍫 Chocolate Sales Analysis – Technical Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing chocolate sales performance using exploratory data analysis (EDA) techniques. The objective is to uncover key revenue drivers, identify sales trends, evaluate regional performance, and generate actionable business recommendations.

Through data cleaning, aggregation, and visualization, this analysis transforms raw transactional data into strategic business insights.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing products and countries
- Evaluate sales trends over time
- Generate business-driven recommendations

---

## 🛠 Data Processing Steps

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning
- Converted `Date` column into datetime format
- Checked and handled missing values
- Removed duplicate records
- Verified data types for numerical consistency

### 2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Total revenue calculation
- Sales distribution by country
- Product performance ranking
- Time-series trend visualization

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### Country Performance
- Australia recorded the highest sales performance.
- The leading product in Australia is **50% Dark Bites**, generating approximately **89 Thousand** in sales.

### 🏆 Product Performance
- **Ches Borneli** generated approximately **0.32 Million** in total revenue, making it one of the strongest contributors.

### 📈 Sales Trend
- Overall sales trends remain relatively stable.
- A noticeable increase occurred on **June 30, 2022**, indicating a possible seasonal or promotional effect.

### ⭐ Best Seller Potential
- **Smooth Silky Salty** stands out as a high-performing premium product.
- It combines strong sales performance with premium positioning, making it strategically valuable.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

### 1️⃣ Prioritize Top-Performing Products
The company should prioritize marketing efforts and inventory allocation for top-performing chocolate products, as they significantly contribute to total revenue.

Optimizing social media promotions for these products could further boost overall sales performance.

### 2️⃣ Evaluate Low-Performing Countries
Lower-performing countries should be reassessed to evaluate:

- Local market potential
- Competitive landscape
- Field conditions
- Distribution coverage

Targeted social media campaigns may help expand the customer base, including younger market segments.
